  • Personal brand equity — aset tak terlihat terpenting
  • Smartphone 48MP + ring light + mikrofon eksternal
  • CapCut, Canva Pro, Notion, Later — core productivity stack
  • Modal minimal: Rp100.000/bulan (kuota + HP)

Key Activities
  • Riset tren & ideasi konten — 45 menit/hari

Key Partnerships

  • Platform affiliate & marketplace digital Indonesia

Cost Structure
  • Pre-revenue (0–3 bln): Rp100k/bulan
