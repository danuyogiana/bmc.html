Business Model Canvas – Konten Kreator & Personal Branding

Framework eksekutif premium untuk Gen Z • Dari kreator biasa menjadi aset digital bernilai tinggi

Executive Summary

Personal branding adalah moat kompetitif abad digital. BMC ini memungkinkan Gen Z memulai dengan modal Rp100.000/bulan dan dalam 6 bulan mencapai pendapatan Rp3–12 juta/bulan melalui diversifikasi 5+ revenue streams. Berbasis data dari 40+ kreator sukses.

+64% Engagement 5–6 jam/hari Rp3–12 jt (bulan ke-6)

Business Model Canvas

9 Strategic Pillars

1. Customer Segments

  • Aspiring Creator (62%) – ingin memulai, bingung strategi
  • Productivity Seeker (23%) – self-improvement
  • Trend Observer (15%) – early adopter tren
  • 🎯 Segmentasi psikografis berbasis pain point

2. Value Propositions

  • Authenticity-first storytelling → trust & loyalty
  • Actionable framework (template siap pakai)
  • Consistency without burnout – sistem sustainable
  • ★ MOAT Personal brand aset eksklusif

3. Channels

  • TikTok, IG Reels, YT Shorts – viral loop
  • YouTube / LinkedIn – credibility
  • Komunitas eksklusif (Telegram/Discord)
  • Gumroad / Tokopedia – monetisasi

4. Customer Relationships

  • 💬 Hyper-personalized DM (balas 50+/hari)
  • 🔄 Co-creation loop: polling topik mingguan
  • 🎁 Unboxed value: template gratis
  • 🔓 Transparansi monetisasi → Gen Z menghargai

5. Revenue Streams

  • 📢 Brand endorsement: Rp400k–7jt/konten
  • 🔗 Affiliate marketing: komisi 10–30%
  • 📦 Digital product: Rp25k–150k
  • 🎓 Mini-course: Rp49k–99k/bulan
  • 🎤 1-on-1 coaching: Rp250k–750k/sesi
  • Proyeksi bulan ke-6: Rp3–12 juta

6. Key Resources

  • 🧠 Personal brand equity (aset tak terlihat)
  • 📱 Smartphone 48MP + ring light + mic
  • 🎬 Tools: CapCut, Canva Pro, Notion, Later
  • ✅ Minimal modal: Rp100.000/bulan (kuota+HP)

7. Key Activities

  • 📈 Riset tren & ideation (45 menit/hari)
  • 🎥 Produksi konten (2–3 jam/hari)
  • 📅 Scheduling & publishing (30 menit/hari)
  • 💬 Engagement & live interaksi (1,5 jam/hari)

8. Key Partnerships

  • 🤝 Kreator satu tier (cross-promotion)
  • 🏢 Brand lokal UMKM (endorse barter+fee)
  • 📱 Platform affiliate & marketplace
  • 🌐 Komunitas kreator offline/online

9. Cost Structure

  • Fase pre-revenue (0–3 bln): Rp100k/bulan
  • Fase early revenue (3–6 bln): ±Rp530k/bulan
  • Burn rate ideal < 30% dari pendapatan
  • Reinvestasi ke tools produktivitas
